This app uses the Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK. By using this app, you consent to the collection of certain device data as outlined below for advertising and analytics purposes.

Information Collection and Use

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you automatically. Additionally, specific user-triggered features require data access:

  • Audio Data: This app includes a group chat feature that allows users to send voice messages. To enable this, the app requires access to your device's microphone. Audio is recorded only when you actively use this feature and is processed solely to facilitate communication within the game.
  • Approximate Location: Used by advertising networks (AdMob) for serving relevant, localized advertisements.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID and App Set ID are collected for fraud prevention, analytics, and serving personalized ads.
  • App Performance & Log Data: In case of an error, data including your device IP address, device name, and crash logs is collected through third-party tools to ensure game stability.

App Permissions

To provide advanced AI functionalities, social features, and deliver advertisements, this app requests the following device permissions:

  • Record Audio (RECORD_AUDIO): Specifically required for sending voice messages in the group chat functionality. The app only accesses the microphone when you actively trigger the recording feature.
  • Internet Access (INTERNET): Required to execute cloud-based AI calculations, fetch game updates, and load advertisements from external servers.
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Used to check connectivity for LAN multiplayer and to ensure a smooth user experience when loading online features.
